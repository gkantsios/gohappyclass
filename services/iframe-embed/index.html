HOW IT WORKS

From kickoff to live in three steps.

The same fast-track setup as our WordPress plugin - just without the WordPress.

1

We meet

A quick call to see how you want to display your classes - by location, by age, by program, or however your studio thinks about it.

2

We set you up

We help you set up your first calendar and then give you access to your account. From there, you are able to create as many different calendars as you may need.

3

You paste & go live

We hand you a single iframe snippet. You paste it into your site. Classes appear, in sync with Jackrabbit, ready for parents to enroll.
